Nekoin's Core Features

01
FEATURE

NEKOIN Quests: Gamification for Good

NEKOIN Quests invite users to participate in a variety of activities, from learning modules about blockchain technology and animal welfare to community challenges that support real-world causes. These quests not only engage but also educate the community, rewarding participation with NEKOIN tokens, exclusive NFTs, and special recognition.

02
FEATURE

Cat Caf Virtual Spaces

The Cat Caf Virtual Spaces are digital havens for NEKOIN users, offering a platform for connection, education, and relaxation. Here, members can attend virtual meetups, participate in workshops, and enjoy live streams, all within an immersive cat-themed environment. These virtual spaces strengthen the bonds within the NEKOIN community, making the digital experience more personal and meaningful.

03
FEATURE

Adopt-a-Cat NFT Initiative

The Adopt-a-Cat NFT Initiative bridges the gap between the digital and the real, providing tangible support to cats in need. By partnering with animal shelters, NEKOIN creates unique NFTs representing real cats up for adoption. These NFTs not only help fund the care and adoption process but also offer a novel way for the community to engage with and support animal welfare efforts directly.

Nekoin's Tokenomics

NEKOIN's tokenomics are designed to foster a healthy ecosystem, rewarding participation, ensuring project sustainability, and supporting our philanthropic goals. A portion of every transaction fee is allocated to a dedicated charity wallet, funding animal welfare initiatives and supporting our furry friends in need. Additionally, mechanisms for staking and liquidity provision are in place to incentivize long-term holding and contribute to the stability of the NEKOIN market.
